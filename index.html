Visit the World of Jane Austen's Bath

Immerse yourself in the World of Jane Austen.Enjoy an afternoon tea at the Regency tea rooms which even Lady Catherine would approve of, perhaps indulging in some of Anne Elliot's homemade jam and scones. Visit the house where Jane Austen lived. During the Festival, watch His Majesty's Regiment of Foot parade over Poultney bridge. Minuet at the Summer Ball, or in the Assembly Rooms. Dress in period custume for a reception at the Roman Baths.

Who knows you may even meet Mr Darcy himself!
